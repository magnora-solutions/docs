---
title: "Editing your product catalogue"
description: "Keep your product information accurate in Magnora for correct review matching and syndication."
---

Magnora's product catalogue stores information about the products you sell. It's used to match reviews to the right products and to syndicate reviews across storefronts and sales channels like Google Shopping.

When you connect your Shopify store to Magnora, your product information is automatically synced to build your catalogue. It's important to keep this information accurate — an out-of-date catalogue can cause issues with review matching and syncing.

## What's stored in your product catalogue

| Magnora catalogue field | Shopify field | Where to manage |
|---|---|---|
| Title | Title | Shopify |
| Product Variant ID | Product Variant ID | Shopify |
| Brand | Vendor | Shopify |
| SKU | SKU | Shopify |
| GTIN | Barcode | Shopify or Magnora |

## Updating GTIN, Brand, and SKU

For consistency, the GTIN, Brand, and SKU fields can only be updated from your Shopify admin — changes sync to Magnora automatically.

### Updating a small number of products (1–5)

1. Go to **Shopify Admin → Products** and open the product you want to update
2. Edit the **SKU**, **Barcode** (GTIN), and/or **Vendor** (Brand) fields as needed
3. Click **Save**

<Warning>
  Enter only valid GTINs. Magnora validates GTIN data and will reject entries that don't conform to the GS1 standard.
</Warning>

### Updating many products (5+)

1. Go to **Shopify Admin → Products**, select all the products you want to update, and click **Bulk edit**
2. Use the column selector to add the **SKU**, **Barcode**, and **Vendor** fields to the editing view
3. Update the values across your products and click **Save**

## Verifying changes in Magnora

After updating in Shopify, you can confirm the changes have synced by opening the product in the [Products section](https://app.magnora.io/products) of your Magnora admin. Look under **Unique Product Identifiers** to see the updated values.

You can also download a full CSV export of your product catalogue from **Settings → Syndication settings → Manage Catalogue → Generate CSV Export**.
