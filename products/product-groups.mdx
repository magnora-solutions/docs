---
title: "Product groups (bundle & family)"
description: "Share reviews between related products in Magnora using families and bundles."
---

Product groups let you consolidate reviews across products that are related — either because they're the same product listed differently, or because one product is a bundle of others.

<Note>
  Product groups are available on the Core plan and higher.
</Note>

Magnora has two types of product groups:

- **Families** — for products that are the same in reality but listed separately in Shopify (e.g. different colours, subscription vs one-time purchase variants)
- **Bundles** — for pulling reviews up from individual component products into a bundle product

To manage groups, go to [Products → Groups](https://app.magnora.io/products/groups) in your Magnora admin.

## Creating a family

Families share reviews two-ways between all products in the group. This is ideal when the same product exists as multiple SKUs.

1. Go to [Products → Groups](https://app.magnora.io/products/groups) and click **Create Group → Family**
2. Name your family (this is for internal use only — customers won't see it)
3. Add products to the group by either:
   - Clicking **Select products** and manually choosing products
   - Using a product tag to automatically include all products with that tag (tags sync from Shopify automatically)
4. Save the group

<Tip>
  If a product in a family is discontinued, use Shopify's **Draft** or **Archived** status rather than deleting it outright — this keeps the reviews visible on the other products in the family.
</Tip>

## Creating a bundle

Bundles pull reviews up from the individual products in a bundle to aggregate them on the bundle product page.

1. Go to [Products → Groups](https://app.magnora.io/products/groups) and click **Create Group → Bundle**
2. In the top block, select your bundle product
3. In the main section, select all the component products included in that bundle
4. Save the group

## FAQs

**Are family group names visible to customers?**
No — group names are for internal use only and are never shown on your storefront.

**How many products can be in a group?**
Core and Growth plans support up to 100 products per group. Premium plans support up to 1,000 products per group. If you need more, contact [support@magnora.io](mailto:support@magnora.io).

**Reviews aren't showing after I created a group — what do I do?**
It can take a few moments after creating a group for the review section to update due to browser caching. To force a refresh, add `/?magnora-break-cache=true` to the end of your store's URL.
