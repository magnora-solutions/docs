---
title: "Exporting your reviews"
description: "Download your review data as a CSV for reporting, analysis, or migration."
---

You can export your reviews from Magnora at any time — useful for reporting, external analysis, or moving to another platform.

## How to export

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Reviews">
    Head to the [Reviews](https://app.magnora.io/reviews) section in your Magnora admin.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter your data (optional)">
    Use the date range filter to select a specific time period, or click **Custom** to enter your own date range. You can also filter by rating, response status, product, and more to narrow down what gets included in the export.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Export">
    Click the **Export** button in the top right corner of the reviews page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and configure">
    You'll be asked to confirm the export and optionally include additional data fields.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download your file">
    Magnora will generate the export and email it to you when it's ready. It will also appear at the top of your Reviews section — click **Download** to save the CSV file.
  </Step>
</Steps>
