---
title: "Can a customer edit their review?"
description: "Learn how customers can update or delete reviews they've submitted in Magnora."
---

Yes — customers can edit or delete any review they've left through Magnora.

## How it works

Customers use the **Edit reviews** link, which you can find in your admin under [Forms](https://app.magnora.io/forms). You'll also see a shortcut to this link when you open an individual review in the [Reviews](https://app.magnora.io/reviews) section.

The same link works for all customers, so it's a good idea for your support team to keep it on hand — they can share it directly in conversations when a customer asks to update or remove a review.

When a customer visits the link, they'll verify their identity via a passwordless authentication (a one-time code sent to their email). Once confirmed, they'll see all reviews they've previously submitted and can make any changes they'd like.

## FAQs

**Will I be notified when a customer edits or deletes a review?**
No. Magnora does not send a notification when a customer edits or deletes a review.

**Is a deleted review removed from all displays?**
Yes. When a customer deletes a review, it's removed from all Magnora widgets across your site — product pages, storewide review pages, carousels, and anywhere else it appeared. The same applies when a review is updated. Due to caching, it can take a few minutes for the change to be reflected on your live site.
