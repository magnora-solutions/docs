---
title: "Replying to reviews"
description: "Write public responses to customer reviews from your Magnora admin."
---

Replying to reviews is one of the best ways to show customers you're listening — whether you're thanking someone for a kind word or adding context to a critical review.

## How to reply

1. Go to [Reviews](https://app.magnora.io/reviews) in your Magnora admin
2. Click on a review to open it
3. Write your response in the reply text box
4. Check **Notify customer of your response** if you want the customer to receive an email about your reply
5. Click **Reply** — your response is immediately published on your storefront

## Editing a reply

To edit an existing reply, click the **edit** icon next to it, update the text, and click **Save**.

## Tips for effective replies

- Thank customers for positive reviews — it reinforces loyalty and shows you value feedback
- For critical reviews, be calm and constructive. Acknowledge the issue, explain what happened if relevant, and offer to resolve it
- Keep replies concise — shoppers reading reviews will also see your responses
