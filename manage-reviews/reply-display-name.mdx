---
title: "Changing the reply display name"
description: "Control whether public review replies appear under a team member's name or your store name."
---

When you reply to a review, Magnora shows a name next to your response on the storefront. You can choose how that name is displayed.

## How to change the setting

Go to **Settings → Review Reply Settings** in your Magnora admin ([app.magnora.io/settings/review-replies](https://app.magnora.io/settings/review-replies)).

You can set replies to be published under:

- **A team member's name** — the name of the logged-in user who writes the reply
- **Your store name** — a consistent brand name shown for all replies, regardless of who wrote them

<Note>
  Changing this setting only applies to new replies going forward. Existing published replies will not be updated.
</Note>
