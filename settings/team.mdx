---
title: "Team members"
description: "Invite team members, manage access, and transfer ownership of your Magnora account."
---

You can manage who has access to your Magnora account from the [Team page](https://app.magnora.io/settings/team) in your admin.

## Inviting a new user

Enter the team member's email address and click **Invite user**. They'll receive an invitation to join your Magnora organisation.

<Note>
  Users must be invited by the account owner — they cannot join an organisation through the standard sign-up flow on their own.
</Note>

## Transferring ownership

If you're the account owner, you can transfer ownership to any existing user in your organisation from the [Team page](https://app.magnora.io/settings/team).

## Removing a user

Account owners can remove any user from the organisation at any time from the same Team settings page.

## FAQs

**What if the account owner no longer works at the company?**

If the account owner is no longer reachable, the fastest path forward is to reset their password using the [forgot password flow](https://app.magnora.io/login). Make sure emails to the owner's address are forwarded to an inbox you can access before doing this.

Once you're in, you can either transfer ownership to another user or invite a new user and assign ownership to them.
