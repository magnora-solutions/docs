---
title: "Notification emails"
description: "Control which emails Magnora sends you for new reviews and performance updates."
---

Notification emails keep you up to date with what's happening in your Magnora account without needing to log in. They're managed at a per-user level and are turned off by default.

## Types of notifications

**New review emails**
Receive an email each time a customer leaves a review. You can filter these by star rating — for example, only receive a notification when a 1-star or 5-star review comes in.

**Digest emails**
A summary of your review programme performance, sent daily or weekly — whichever you prefer. You can also set the time of day you'd like to receive these.

## Managing your notifications

Notification settings are available on all plans. Go to [Settings → Notifications](https://app.magnora.io/settings/notifications) to turn them on and customise your preferences.
