If you haven't already, create your Magnora account — it only takes a minute:

[https://app.magnora.io/signup](https://app.magnora.io/signup)

Once your account is created, a setup guide will walk you through your first steps. The sections below cover the most important things to get done early.

## What's next?

After creating your account, the recommended order is:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your store">
    Install the Magnora app from the Shopify App Store and follow the prompts to connect it to your store.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import any existing reviews">
    If you're moving over from another platform, [import your reviews](/getting-started/importing-reviews) before doing anything else so you don't start from zero.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your flows">
    [Configure your flows](/getting-started/setting-up-flows) so Magnora starts automatically sending review requests to customers after they purchase.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add review displays to your store">
    [Add widgets](/getting-started/adding-displays) to your product pages and homepage so shoppers can see your reviews right away.
  </Step>
</Steps>
