---
title: "Displays not showing on your website"
description: "Troubleshoot why your Magnora review widgets aren't appearing on your live site."
---

If you've added the Magnora blocks in the theme editor but your review displays aren't showing on your live site, the most common cause is the **app embed not being enabled**.

## Check the app embed

1. Open your Shopify theme customizer
2. Click the **App embeds** tab in the sidebar
3. Search for **Magnora**
4. Make sure the toggle is turned **on**
5. Click **Save**

Once the app embed is enabled, the Magnora blocks should start loading on your pages.

## Still not showing?

If the app embed is enabled and the displays still aren't appearing, try the following:

- **Hard refresh your browser** — some display changes require a cache clear to reflect. Try `Cmd + Shift + R` (Mac) or `Ctrl + Shift + R` (Windows).
- **Check you saved the theme** — unsaved changes in the theme editor won't apply to your live site.
- **Check the correct theme is active** — make sure you enabled the app embed on your live theme, not a draft.
- **Verify the blocks are added** — go back into the theme editor and confirm the Magnora blocks are present on the pages where you expect to see reviews.

If you've checked all of the above and reviews still aren't showing, reach out to [support@magnora.io](mailto:support@magnora.io) and we'll help you troubleshoot.
