---
title: "Widget overview"
description: "Understand Magnora's display widgets and when to use each one."
---

Magnora offers four display widgets. Each serves a different purpose — here's what they do and where to use them.

## Product reviews widget

Displays reviews for a **specific product** and includes a **Write a Review** button so customers can submit a review directly.

Because it's tied to a single product, this widget should only be placed on **product pages**.

## Storewide reviews widget

Displays both product and store reviews in two separate tabs:

- **Product reviews** — shows reviews from all products across your store
- **Store reviews** — shows store-level reviews and includes a **Write a Review** button

Customers can only submit **store reviews** through this widget. It doesn't support product review submissions since it isn't linked to any specific product.

This widget can be placed anywhere on your site, but it's most commonly used on a **dedicated reviews page**.

## Review carousel

A scrollable carousel that highlights a selection of your reviews. It can be placed anywhere on your site — homepage, about page, landing pages, etc.

The carousel does **not** include a review submission button.

## UGC gallery

Displays a gallery of photos and videos from your product reviews. Can be placed anywhere on your site.

The UGC gallery does **not** include a review submission button.

---

| Widget | Best for | Write a review? |
|---|---|---|
| Product reviews | Product pages | Yes (product reviews) |
| Storewide reviews | Dedicated reviews page | Yes (store reviews only) |
| Review carousel | Homepage, any page | No |
| UGC gallery | Homepage, any page | No |
