---
title: "Incentives"
description: "Reward customers with a discount code when they leave a review to increase submission rates."
---

Magnora can automatically generate a unique, one-time-use discount code for customers who leave a product review. Magnora handles everything in the background — checking eligibility, creating the code in your Shopify discount engine, and delivering it to the customer.

## Types of incentives

You can set up two types:

- **Standard incentive** — given to any customer who leaves a review, regardless of whether they include media
- **Media incentive** — given to customers who include a photo or video in their review. If a customer qualifies for both, they only receive the better of the two.

For each type, you can choose from:

- Free shipping coupon
- Dollar-off coupon
- Percentage-off coupon

<Note>
  Incentives only apply to product reviews. Store reviews (seller ratings) cannot be incentivised.
</Note>

## How to set up incentives

Go to [Incentives](https://app.magnora.io/incentives) in your Magnora admin:

1. Choose **Standard Incentive** or **Media Incentive**
2. Select the discount type
3. Enter the value (for fixed or percentage discounts)
4. Choose whether to offer the incentive to unverified buyers — we recommend leaving this off
5. Click **Add incentive**

<Tip>
  Once incentives are live, update the subject line or body of your review request messages to let customers know a reward is waiting — this can meaningfully improve your submission rate.
</Tip>

## Tiered incentives

You can run both a standard and a media incentive at the same time, with the media incentive offering a higher reward. For example:

- Standard: 10% off for any review
- Media: 15% off for reviews that include a photo or video

A customer will only ever receive one incentive — whichever is the best they qualify for.

## How discount codes are created in Shopify

Magnora uses your Shopify discount engine to generate the codes. You'll see pools of incentive codes in your **Shopify Admin → Discounts** section — they're easy to spot because they use the prefix **MG-**.

## What customers see

**During the review form:** If you have a media incentive active, Magnora automatically adds a prompt on the "Add media" step of the form letting customers know they can earn a higher reward by including a photo or video.

**After submitting the review:** The discount code is shown on the confirmation page immediately after submission.

**By email:** Magnora also sends customers a follow-up email with their discount code. For this to send automatically, make sure your **Review submitted actions** flow is turned on and includes the incentive email as an action. You can edit the email template from your [Templates page](https://app.magnora.io/flows/templates).

## Sending incentive emails through an integration

If you're using Klaviyo or another integration to manage post-review emails, you can trigger the incentive email using the `Magnora - Review Submitted` event. Contact [support@magnora.io](mailto:support@magnora.io) for setup guidance for your specific integration.

## FAQs

**Do discount codes expire?**
By default, Magnora incentive codes don't have an expiry date. They remain active until manually disabled in your Shopify admin.

**Can incentive codes be combined with other discounts?**
By default, Magnora codes are set to not stack with other discounts — this prevents customers from combining them with existing promotions for an unintended total discount.

**Does a customer get multiple codes if they review multiple products from one order?**
No. Only one incentive is rewarded per order, even if the customer reviews multiple items.
