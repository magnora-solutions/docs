---
title: "Additional questions"
description: "Add custom or preset questions to your review form to capture richer customer feedback."
---

Additional questions let you collect more than just a star rating and written review. Once added, they become part of the review submission experience for customers, and public responses are displayed dynamically on your product page — helping new shoppers make faster decisions.

<Note>
  Additional questions are available on the Growth plan and higher.
</Note>

Head to **Forms** → [Additional questions](https://app.magnora.io/forms/questions) in your Magnora admin to get started. You can choose between preset questions and custom questions.

## Preset questions

Preset questions are ready-to-use and can be added instantly. They cover common categories including:

- **Product attributes** — sizing, quality, comfort
- **Customer details** — age, skin type, hair concerns

Preset questions support localization, so if you're using Magnora's translation features, they'll be translated automatically.

### How to add a preset question

1. Click **Add Question** and select **Preset question**
2. Choose your question from the list
3. Set **Response Sharing** to **Public** (responses appear on your live site) or **Private** (only visible to you in the admin)
4. Click **Use template**
5. Under **Product assignment**, choose whether to apply the question to **All products** or **Some products** (using conditions)
6. Click **Save**

## Custom questions

Custom questions let you tailor exactly what you ask. There are four types:

| Type | Description |
|---|---|
| **Single-select** | Customers pick one answer from a list. Becomes a filter on your product page. |
| **Multi-select** | Customers pick multiple answers. Also becomes a filter on your product page. |
| **Written answer** | An open-ended text field — customers write their own response. |
| **Linear scale** | Customers rate product traits on a 5-point scale (e.g. fit, quality, comfort). |

### How to create a custom question

1. Click **Add Question** and select the type of custom question you want
2. Enter a **Display name** — this is what shoppers see on your product page (e.g. "Skin Concerns")
3. Write the **Question** you want to ask customers
4. Add your **Response options** (for single-select and multi-select questions)
5. Set **Response sharing** to **Public** or **Private**

<Warning>
  Once a question is set to Private, it cannot be switched to Public later. Choose carefully.
</Warning>

6. Under **Product assignment**, choose which products the question applies to
7. Click **Save**

Once saved, the question is automatically included in forms and displays. To preview it in context, go to **Forms → Preferences → Preview** and select a product the question is assigned to.

### A note on imports

If you're recreating custom questions from another platform as part of a migration, make sure the **Display name** and **Response options** in Magnora exactly match the values in your export file — including capitalisation. This ensures the data maps correctly on import.
