title: "Previewing your review form"
description: "See exactly what customers experience when they submit a review in Magnora."
---

Before sharing your review form with customers, you can preview what they'll see — including any custom questions you've added.

## How to preview

In your Magnora admin, go to **Forms** → [Preferences](https://app.magnora.io/forms/preferences).

Under the **Preview** section you have two options:

**Product-specific preview**
Use the dropdown to select a product and preview the form as it would appear for that product. This is especially useful when you've set up custom questions that only apply to certain products — you can verify they're appearing correctly.

**Generic preview**
Click the link under **Generic Preview** to open the review submission experience using sample data. This gives you a general sense of the form layout and flow without needing to select a specific product.

## What you can check

- The order and wording of your review questions
- Which custom or preset questions appear for a given product
- How the form looks and flows from start to finish
- That any required fields are set up correctly

## After previewing

If you need to make changes to your questions, head to [Forms → Additional questions](https://app.magnora.io/forms/questions). Once you're happy with the setup, the form will automatically be used for all future review submissions.
